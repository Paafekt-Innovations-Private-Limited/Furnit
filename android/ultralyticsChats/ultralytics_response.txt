To ensure the 3D room remains visible during furniture detection, you should avoid the overhead of a new Activity. Loading Gaussian Splatting data in a new WebView triggers a full re-initialization of the rendering engine, causing the "grey screen" lag. Recommended Strategy Single Activity Architecture: Do not start FurnitureFitActivity . Instead, keep SharpRoomActivity active and add the progress UI as an overlay View or Fragment within the same layout. This prevents the WebView from being destroyed and recreated. Layered UI: Place your progress bar in a FrameLayout or ConstraintLayout with a higher z-index (elevation) than the WebView. Set its initial visibility to GONE and toggle it to VISIBLE when the "brain" button is tapped. Background Processing: Run your "SmartyPants" detection logic using a ViewModel and lifecycleScope .
