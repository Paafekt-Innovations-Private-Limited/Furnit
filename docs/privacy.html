Paafekt Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 May 2026

Paafekt Inc. ("Paafekt," "we," "us") operates the Paafekt mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information we collect

Account information. When you sign in, we collect your phone number to authenticate you and create an account. We assign you an internal user ID. Authentication is provided by Google Firebase Authentication, which also processes a device installation identifier for abuse detection.

Room and furniture scans. The App captures camera frames, motion data, and depth/AR data to reconstruct rooms and detect furniture. This processing happens on your device using on-device neural networks. We do not upload your raw room photos or scans to our servers as a condition of using core App features.

Saved rooms. Rooms you save are stored locally on your device. If you explicitly choose to share or back up a room, the relevant data is transmitted to our servers solely to fulfill that request.

Support communications. If you email support@paafekt.com, we retain your message and email address to respond to you.

2. How we use information

  • To authenticate you and operate your account
  • To provide room scanning, measurement, and AR fit features
  • To respond to support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Information we do not collect

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your data for third-party advertising.
  • We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

4. Data sharing

We share data only with:

  • Service providers who host our backend or send authentication codes (e.g., Google Firebase, SMS providers), under contract and only to deliver the App.
  • Law enforcement when required by valid legal process.

5. Data retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. Saved rooms remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App. When you delete your account, we delete your associated server-side data.

6. Your rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and all associated server-side data at any time from Settings > Account > Delete Account.
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing support@paafekt.com.

Depending on your location (EEA, UK, California, etc.), you may have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, or other laws. Contact us to exercise them.

7. Children

Paafekt is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures (TLS in transit, access controls) to protect data. No system is 100% secure.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced in the App or by email.
